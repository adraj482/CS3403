↓↓  Recipes for Making Coffee   ↓↓

    INGRIDIENTS:

  • 1 Cup of Milk
  • 1 Teaspoon Sugar
  • 1 Teaspoon Coffee Powder

    INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Light the stove
  2. Mix all the ingridients in above quantity and put over stove in a pan.
  3. Wait for 5 minutes for coffee to get heated.
  4. Coffee is ready to be Served
